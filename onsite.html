On-Site Bootcamp

After completing the preparatory period, 21 students showed the skills required to advance to the On-Site Bootcamp whose objective was to delve deeper into the LNP/BP technological stack through practical sessions. Students gained knowledge about cryptography and cybersecurity, mining, and the lightning network thanks to the mentorship of experts such as Peter Todd and Adam Gibson.
