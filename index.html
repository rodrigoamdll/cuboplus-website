Our Educational Program

This year the cubo+ program was divided into the following phases

Preparatory Period

The preparatory period, which ran from mid-April to early June, consisted of online sessions. The objective was to share with students the role of Bitcoin and its impact on society.

More info

On-Site Bootcamp

The on-site bootcamp took place the first two weeks of July focused on delving deeper into the LNP/BP technology stack through hands-on sessions, all while students learned from world-class experts.

More info
Tuscany Bootcamp

The top candidates from the on-site bootcamp had the opportunity to travel to Italy and Switzerland to participate in the Tuscany Lightning Bootcamp during the first weeks of October.

More info

About
CUBO+

CUBO+ Educational Program is an initiative designed by and for Salvadorans, aimed at training the upcoming generation of Salvadoran programmers. These students were equipped with the skills and knowledge needed to shape the future of Bitcoin adoption and develop innovative solutions that would drive economic growth within the local community.
